Acme Robotics — Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible to work remotely up to three days per week, subject to manager approval. Employees in roles requiring on-site equipment (hardware assembly, lab testing) are not eligible for remote work on lab days.

2. Core Hours
Remote employees are expected to be reachable between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM in their local time zone, regardless of which days they work remotely. Meetings should be scheduled within core hours whenever possible.

3. Equipment and Expenses
Acme Robotics provides a one-time home office stipend of $500 for new remote employees, covering items such as a monitor, keyboard, or ergonomic chair. Internet costs are not reimbursed. Employees must use company-issued laptops for all work; personal devices may not be used to access internal systems.

4. Security Requirements
Remote employees must connect to internal systems only through the company VPN. Screens must be locked whenever a device is left unattended. Working from public spaces such as cafes is discouraged for any task involving confidential client data.

5. Performance Expectations
Remote work arrangements are reviewed quarterly alongside regular performance reviews. Managers may revoke remote work privileges for individual employees if response times, meeting attendance, or deliverable quality decline.

6. Requesting a Change
Employees wishing to increase their remote days beyond three per week should submit a request through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Fully remote arrangements are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by the department head.

For questions about this policy, contact the People Operations team.
